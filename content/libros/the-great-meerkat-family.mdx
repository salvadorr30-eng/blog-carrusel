## Synopsis

In the vast African savanna lives **a very special colony**: the meerkat family. Although they are small, they always find a way to take care of each other, watch over the little ones, and face together the surprises of each new day.

Through stories full of humor, warmth and lessons, this comic invites us to discover the life of meerkats and, along the way, **the value of family, teamwork and friendship**.

## Who is this comic for

- Children aged **6 to 10** who are starting to read on their own.
- Families looking for a book to read together before bedtime.
- Teachers and librarians introducing comics as a reading tool.
- Animal and nature lovers.

## What's inside

- Full-color illustrations on every page.
- Self-contained stories that are easy to follow.
- Endearing characters kids will remember.
- Positive values without preaching: cooperation, empathy, resilience.
